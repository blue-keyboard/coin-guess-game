Coin Guess
How to play

Game for 2-4 players.

Once you have your group to play, all of you are ready and the number of rounds has been decided, each player can choose from 0 to 3 coins to hold in his hand. Once all players has made their decision, in clockwise order, each player will try to guess what's the total sum of the coins. Very important, you can't repeat the same guess!

If a player is correct, he/she steps out of the round and gets the assigned points for being the first one right. The round continues until only one is left, who would lose points, but also decides who goes first next round.

If no one is correct, again in clockwise order, the next player is who makes his/her guess first.

Once you play a bit, is very intuitive how it works.

Have fun!
